TEDxUniMelb is an independently organised club run by students at the University of Melbourne and affiliated to the not-for-profit organisation, TED. In the spirit of TED's philosophy of “ideas worth spreading”, we believe in the power of innovative ideas to have a profound and positive influence on society.
